Show Current Tab Trackers:

Shows the number of tracking links found in the current tab on the Trocker icon.
(The number includes invisible trackers loaded in the background)

Make Tracking Images Visible:

Hidden Tracking images will become visible as (http links) or (https links)
This will not affect how other people will see the tracked emails you send them.
It only helps you visually track where tracker softwares inject their tracking images.
